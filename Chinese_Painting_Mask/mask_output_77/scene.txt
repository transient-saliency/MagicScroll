Chu rice immortal likes living in a tower
 Planting wu trees on a high ridge to form a refined cottage
 Looking down at the Red County, entertaining guest frogs and birds
 East and west, leaping around in the sky
 The belly stores ten thousand volumes, the chest hangs the Hu text
 Discussing how to settle the nine canals
 Lift your beard, send your gaze to tour the eight regions, and desire to call on Yu Shun to drift to Cangwu
